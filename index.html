Popular Places

Cebu, an island province in the Philippines, is rich in history and culture. Here are some of its most popular historical places:

  • 1st

    Magellan's Cross

    Historical site marking the arrival of Christianity in the Philippines.

  • 2nd

    Santo Niño

    Home to the revered Santo Niño statue, Cebu’s spiritual center

  • 3rd

    Fort San Pedro

    The oldest and smallest fort, built to protect Cebu.

  • 4th

    Colon Street

    The oldest street in the Philippines, named after Christopher Columbus.

  • 5th

    Yap Sandiego

    Traditional Spanis era, reflecting Cebus old architecture and lifestyle.

  • 6th

    Cathedral

    The Philippines' oldest cathedral, central to Cebu’s Catholic heritage.

  • 7th

    Casa Gorordo

    Displays 19th-century Filipino elite life with historical artifacts and furniture.

  • 8th

    Taoist Temple

    Worship site for Taoism followers, featuring intricate architecture.

  • 9th

    Mactan Shrine

    Commemorates the Battle of Mactan and Lapulapu's victory over Magellan.
